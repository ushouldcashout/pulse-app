Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Pulse DeFi predictions.

What is Pulse DeFi Predictions? ▼

Pulse DeFi Predictions is a decentralized prediction market platform built on the Ink ecosystem. Users can predict outcomes of DeFi metrics and events, placing testnet ETH bets on outcomes. Markets resolve automatically using on-chain data and winners share the losing pool through a parimutuel system.

How do I place a bet? ▼

To place a bet: 1) Connect your wallet using the button in the top right, 2) Select a market and choose YES or NO, 3) Enter your bet amount in testnet ETH, 4) Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your bet will be locked until the market resolves.

What is the parimutuel payout system? ▼

In a parimutuel system, winners share the losing side's pool proportional to their stake. If you bet 2 ETH and win on a side with 10 ETH total bet, you get 20% of the losing side's pool (minus the 10% Pulse rake). This creates fair payouts based on prediction accuracy and stake size.

How often do markets resolve? ▼

Markets vary in duration based on the event they predict. Most weekly markets launch Monday at 00:00 UTC and resolve Sunday at 23:59 UTC. Some markets resolve immediately after their triggering events. Check the countdown timer on each market card for the exact resolution time.

How do markets resolve automatically? ▼

Markets use oracle feeds that pull real on-chain data from verified protocol sources. At resolution time, these oracles automatically determine the outcome based on whether the metric exceeded the threshold. No manual intervention is needed - resolution is deterministic and transparent.

What is testnet ETH and can I withdraw? ▼

Testnet ETH (Sepolia ETH) is free test currency that has no real value. It's used for testing smart contracts and dApps without spending real money. You cannot convert testnet ETH to real ETH, but you can get free testnet ETH from faucets. Pulse operates on testnet to allow risk-free learning and testing.

How do I connect my wallet? ▼

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right. This will open a dialog to connect MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other Web3 wallets. Select your preferred wallet and follow the prompts to authorize the connection. You'll see your wallet address after connection succeeds.

Is there a rake or fee? ▼

Yes, Pulse takes a 10% rake from winning pools. This means if you win, you receive 90% of your winnings, with 10% going to Pulse for operations, development, and ecosystem support. This fee structure is standard in prediction markets and ensures long-term sustainability.
